Basic Information and Core Architecture
Metaview is a specialized Al platform built for the recruiting vertical, focusing on

transforming ephemeral interview data into structured intelligence. The official tool name is

"Metaview," operated by Metaview AI Ltd (legal entity based in the UK). The primary

website is https://www.metaview.ai/, with a logo available at

As its primary category, Metaview fits squarely in Assessment & Interviewing, with

extensions into sourcing and reporting. A short description: "Al-driven interview

intelligence for automated notes, sourcing, and analytics in recruiting."


For a detailed long description: Metaview addresses the data deficit in talent acquisition by

capturing, analyzing, and operationalizing interview conversations. Unlike horizontal tools,

it's purpose-built for high-stakes assessments, using LLMs fine-tuned on recruitment

data, ASR for transcription, and speaker diarization to differentiate interviewer and

candidate inputs. The architecture revolves around four pillars: Al Notetaker (autonomous

call joining and semantic parsing into categories like technical skills and cultural fit), Al

Sourcing (intake-to-search loops that translate briefing nuances into candidate scans), Al

Reports (analytics for talk ratios, question consistency, and topic coverage), and Al Job

Posts (generative drafts aligned with intake transcripts for inclusive language). Multi-

source capability integrates resumes, JDs, rubrics, and internal knowledge bases for

contextual insights, enabling fact-checking and holistic summaries. This shifts recruiting

from assisted to autonomous, with integrations closing the loop to ATS systems. Target

users include in-house recruiters and hiring managers at mid-to-large tech and enterprise

firms, where cognitive load from dual-tasking (engagement vs. documentation) is acute.

Main use cases encompass interview documentation (offloading notes for better rapport),

standardization (immutable records for calibration), sourcing automation (feedback loops

refining searches), and operational analytics (flagging rogue interviewers or imbalances).

The Al approach emphasizes agentic workflows, with privacy-first features like Transient

Mode ensuring real-time processing without storage. Differentiators include ATS Magic for

scorecard autofill and evidence mapping, plus a privacy moat via UK data residency and

compliance certifications.
